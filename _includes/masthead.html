Sam Gold

I’m a Product Manager at Asana, where I spend my time building a product that empowers teams to do great things together.

 Before Asana, I spent time at Stanford working towards B.S. and M.S. degrees in Management Science and Engineering. At Stanford, I spent time as CEO of nonprofit organization Governor’s Corner Dining, where I managed 4 FTE and dozens of part-time foodservice employees. I also spent time on the product team at Axon and in supply chain at Forbes Marshall in Pune, India.

Before Stanford, I ran a subscription box company called Yumvelope, shipping a monthly selection of awesome snacks that subscribers loved. Less than 0.5% of subscribers returned their orders and we received press mentions in The New York Times, Entrepreneur, and Sierra.
